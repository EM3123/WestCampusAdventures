🏮Chinatown Development Initiative

We're working to establish a Youth Commission dedicated to the downtown development of Sacramento's new Chinatown. Our mission is to bridge generations by connecting elderly, primarily non-English speaking Chinese residents with young Asian American students.

  • Intergenerational Festivals & Events — Celebrate traditions and create new memories.
  • Community Service Projects — Support the elderly and preserve heritage.
  • Youth Leadership Opportunities — Organize, plan, and make a real impact.

Get Involved: We're seeking passionate young leaders to help plan and organize cultural festivals, workshops, and outreach that celebrate our rich Chinese heritage and strengthen community bonds.

🌟Adventure Club Mission

The Adventure Club inspires students to explore, challenge themselves, and develop leadership and teamwork skills through outdoor activities, adventurous experiences, and community engagement. We foster personal growth, resilience, and a spirit of curiosity through hands-on experiences across Sacramento.

  • Outdoor Challenges — Kayaking, hiking, waterpark races, and more!
  • Friendship & Teamwork — Build connections with new friends.
  • Grow Your Confidence — Step outside your comfort zone and lead.
